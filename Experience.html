Astar Cafe Tenom

Address: Lot 13A, Ground Floor, Orchard Plaza, Tenom, Malaysia

Position: Waiter

Duration: June 2021-July 2021

-Take orders from customers, written down and communicate them to the kitchen or bar.

-Responsible for setting up tables, clearing dishes, and maintaining a clean and organized dining area.

-Responsible for recommending menu items or specials.

-Handle the billing process, including presenting the check, processing payments, and providing change.

-Interact with customers, answer questions about the menu, and address any concerns or special requests. Providing excellent customer service is a key aspect of the job.

Tenom Coffee Valley (TCV)

Address: Jalan Tun Ahmad Raffai, Tenom, Sabah

Position: Barista

Duration: July 2022-October 2022

-Handle cash transactions, process payments, and provide change.

-Experts in brewing espresso, a concentrated coffee beverage that forms the base for many other coffee drinks.

-Providing excellent customer service is crucial. Baristas interact with customers, take orders, and may offer recommendations based on customer preferences.

-Keep their workstations clean and sanitary. This includes regular cleaning of equipment, utensils, and the overall coffee preparation area.

-Prepare a variety of coffee drinks, including lattes, cappuccinos, macchiatos, and more. Often create intricate and aesthetically pleasing designs, known as latte art, on the surface of the drinks.

Pasaraya Am Tenom

Address: Lot 46-49, Ground Floor, Block H Jalan Binaie Orchard Plaza, Tenom, Sabah

Position: Promoter

Duration: November 2022-Febuary 2023

-Building interaction with customers. This includes answering questions, providing assistance, and offering recommendations based on customer preferences.
